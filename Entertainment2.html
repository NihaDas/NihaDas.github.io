Activities To Do During The Quarantine


We all know Quarantine can be REALLY boring.

So here are some suggestions you could do to pass the time indoors.


Activities you can do

  • You can draw to pass time and to improve your drawing skills.
  • You can learn a new language like French, Russian or even Sign Language. You have a lot of free time, so why not?
  • Learn how to knit and crochet. Knitting and Crochetting is like a form of meditation and doing these can help reduce the anxious quarantine feel. You can make all sort of things such as amigurumi (stuffed animals) and scarves.
  • You should most definitely exercise. Exercising can help you stay fit and healthy. Exercising also helps you regulate your body so you won't be stuck with quarantine fat.
  • You can learn how to cook or bake. Think of all the fun and delicious food you can make. Baguettes, pudding, delicious stone soup. Learn them all. Why not?
  • You should do activites like sudoku, riddles and games to keep your brain active. There's this really good escape room game called Cube Escape that I recommend.
  • How about spending your time watching those shows you've saved to watch on your free time? There are some show suggestions down below but definitely do catch up on shows you want to watch.
  • Spend your time reading a nice luscious book. Books are really really nice. It helps keep you nice and literate during the quarantine.
  • Play patty cake with your family. Family time is important even if you're stuck to each other 24/7.
